Greetings fellow WK users I hope you have been enjoying our services and community content so far! It's been a while since our last update. As of late development has slowed down while focus has been improving the site for better user experience.

## What We're Working On

Since development is still slowly moving along it's best to keep our expectations low for now and be upfront with our users. Updates and fixes may take some time, currently there are 3 things that are of some priority but not essential to the project uptime
- Migrating the old pfps from profiles to the new path we use
- Updating ease of access for admins to edit the database
- Updating old view counts to the new way we count views

Again there are no expected times when these become implemented so if you want a feature implemented feel free to [contribute](https://github.com/orgs/WatermelonKatanaDevs/repositories) to our site
